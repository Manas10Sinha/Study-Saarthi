About Us

Study Saarthi is a premier coaching institute established in 2024, dedicated to provide excellent coaching and guidance to students. The institute offers coaching for students from 6th to 12th grade in Maths and Science, along with Coding classes. The coaching institute is committed for helping students achieve their goals by providing top-quality coaching and mentoring. They have a team of experienced and highly qualified teachers who use innovative and practical teaching methods to help students grasp complex concepts in Maths and Science. The teachers are experts in their respective fields and have years of experience teaching students at various levels.
The institute's primary goal is to provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment that encourages students to ask questions and seek help from their teachers. The teachers are always available to address any doubts or concerns that students may have. They work tirelessly to ensure that each student receives personalized attention and can progress at their own pace. We offer a range of study materials, including work-sheets, e-books, , quizzes and online resources, to help students prepare for their exams thoroughly. The institute also conducts regular tests and assessments to evaluate each student's progress and identify areas that require improvement. The regular tests and assessments also help students become familiar with the exam pattern and build confidence. Overall, Study Saarthi is an excellent institute for students. The institute's experienced teachers, personalized attention, and comprehensive study materials make it an ideal place for students to achieve their academic goals.
